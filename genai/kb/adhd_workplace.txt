Title: Workplace Accommodations for ADHD Adults
Source-URL: https://chadd.org/workplace-success
Fetched-At: 2024-01-30
----------
Effective workplace strategies include structured breaks, noise-cancelling headphones, and task-batching. Research indicates that ADHD employees benefit from flexible scheduling and clear, written instructions. Technology tools like project management software and focus apps show significant productivity improvements. Employers reporting highest success rates implement both environmental modifications and coaching support. Regular feedback and clear deadlines help maintain motivation and task completion.
